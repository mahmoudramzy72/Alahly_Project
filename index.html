About

Al-Ahly Club of Egypt was established in 1907 by many dignitaries in Egypt such as Omar Lotfi Bey, Amin Sami Pasha, Mustafa Kamel and Talaat Harb, Al-Ahly club is considered the largest club in Egypt and Africa due to its winning of 142 local and continental championships, as well as being third in the Club World Cup, as well as being crowned with the Century Club award in Africa.
